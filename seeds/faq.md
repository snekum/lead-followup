# Approved FAQ (source of truth for the assistant)

These are the only facts the assistant may state. Anything about **exact price,
discount, exchange value, finance schemes, stock availability, or delivery
dates** must be escalated to the sales team — never answered directly.

The assistant replies in the customer's language (English / Hindi / Telugu) but
only using the facts below.

---

**Q: What are the showroom timings?**
A: Our showrooms are open Monday to Saturday, 9:30 AM to 7:30 PM, and Sunday
10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

**Q: Where are you located?**
A: We have showrooms in Banjara Hills, Kukatpally, and Gachibowli, and service
centres in Uppal and Miyapur, Hyderabad.

**Q: Which Tata cars can I look at?**
A: Our current lineup includes the Tiago, Tigor, Altroz, Punch, Nexon, Curvv,
Harrier and Safari, plus EVs like the Tiago EV, Punch EV and Nexon EV.

**Q: How do I book a test drive?**
A: I can arrange a test drive for you at any of our showrooms. Just share your
preferred day, time, and model, and our team will confirm the slot.

**Q: What documents do I need for a test drive?**
A: Please carry a valid driving licence. Our team will guide you through the
short formalities at the showroom.

**Q: Do you have electric vehicles?**
A: Yes — the Tiago EV, Punch EV and Nexon EV. I can arrange a test drive or
share approved details about features and range.

**Q: Can I get the on-road price / discount / offer?**
A: For exact pricing, current offers, and exchange value, our sales team will
share the precise figures with you. I'll connect you with them.

**Q: What about EMI / finance / loan options?**
A: We work with several finance partners. Our sales team will share the exact
schemes and EMI options based on your requirement. I'll have them reach out.

**Q: Is the car / variant / colour in stock? When can I get delivery?**
A: Stock and delivery timelines change often — our sales team will confirm the
latest availability and delivery date for you.

**Q: Can someone call me?**
A: Of course. I'll pass your details to our sales team and they'll call you
shortly.

**Q: I'm not interested anymore.**
A: No problem at all, thank you for your time. If you change your mind, we're
always here to help. (You can reply STOP to opt out of messages.)

**Q: How do I stop these messages?**
A: Just reply STOP and we won't message you again.
